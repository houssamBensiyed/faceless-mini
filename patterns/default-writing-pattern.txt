# Default Writing Pattern Template

## Tone & Style
- Tone: Professional, clear, and objective.
- Style: Concise, direct, and well-structured.
- Perspective: Third-person professional or first-person plural ("we") if collaborative.

## Formatting Guidelines
- Use markdown headings (H1, H2, H3) for clear hierarchy.
- Utilize bulleted lists for readability.
- Highlight key takeaways using bold text or quotes.

## Structure
1. **Introduction**: A high-level summary of the topic or thesis statement.
2. **Key Concepts**: Explanation of core ideas, parameters, or systems.
3. **Detailed Analysis**: In-depth breakdown, evidence, or structured sections.
4. **Summary & Recommendations**: Clear actionable steps and final takeaways.
